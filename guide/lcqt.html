Guides

Windows

  • Download windows.zip from this github.
  • Extract the zip file.
  • Open "lunar-client-qt.exe"
  • Download the Weave Loader Agent
  • Head into the agents tab and add the Weave-Loader.jar file you downloaded.
  • Now, go into the general tab and check "Use Custom JRE".
  • Then navigate to C:/Users/YOURPROFILE/.lunarclient/jre/4dcd.../zulu17../bin
  • Now, select javaw.exe.
  • You can now add your mods to the mods tab and launch, just ensure you don't check "Enable Weave".
